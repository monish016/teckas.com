What Is MyOutDesk?

MyOutDesk is a US-based remote staffing company with a strong specialization in real estate virtual assistants. Based on their publicly available information, MyOutDesk has been operating for many years, serves a large base of real estate professionals, and sources talent primarily from the Philippines. They publish subscription-style pricing and have built out deep playbooks and training specific to real estate, mortgage, and insurance workflows. For real estate teams, they are one of the best-known names in the category.

What Is Teckas?

Teckas is a managed remote staffing firm that places India-based professionals with US and Canadian businesses. Our model is broader than any one vertical: we staff real estate VAs, mortgage loan officer assistants, bookkeepers, executive assistants, software developers, operations managers, analysts, and marketing specialists. Pricing is a flat monthly $1,200 to $2,500 all-inclusive, and engagements are month-to-month with a replacement guarantee. We serve real estate heavily but are not limited to it, which is useful for owners who wear multiple hats.

Why People Look for MyOutDesk Alternatives

Typical reasons buyers explore MyOutDesk alternatives:

  • Wanting India-based talent instead of Philippines-based
  • Needing roles outside real estate (e.g., bookkeeping, marketing, tech, operations) without adding a second vendor
  • Preferring month-to-month over longer subscription commitments
  • Looking for lower total monthly cost on comparable roles
  • Specific time zone or shift configuration preferences

What MyOutDesk Does Well

It is worth being direct about this. MyOutDesk has earned a strong reputation in the real estate vertical, and that matters:

  • Deep real estate expertise: Years of vertical focus mean their recruiters know what a good real estate VA looks like, and their training paths reflect that.
  • Playbooks for real estate workflows: Transaction coordination, lead follow-up cadences, MLS data entry, and agent back-office support are well-codified.
  • Established brand in real estate communities: Real estate coaches, brokerages, and team leaders often reference MyOutDesk by name, which builds trust.
  • Mortgage and insurance adjacency: Their specialization extends naturally into lending and insurance operations.

If your entire hiring need sits inside real estate or a closely adjacent vertical, MyOutDesk's specialization is a real asset.

Where Teckas Is Different

Teckas positions differently:

  • India-based talent pool: Large supply of trained professionals across many functions, with strong written English and strong capability in data, finance, and tech-adjacent work.
  • Broader role coverage: One vendor for your real estate VA, your bookkeeper, your marketing coordinator, and (if relevant) a developer or analyst. Useful for small business owners whose needs span multiple functions.
  • Month-to-month: No long commitment required. Scale up or down as your pipeline changes.
  • Flat all-inclusive pricing: $1,200 to $2,500 per month covers salary, benefits, HR, payroll, equipment allowance, and management.
  • US or Canadian shift coverage: Full overlap with your business day.
