Time Etc Alternative: Remote Staffing with Teckas

Weighing Time Etc's hourly VA model against a dedicated full-time staffing partner? Here is a neutral, side-by-side look at how Teckas compares on pricing, talent pool, and which model fits which need.

If you are searching for a Time Etc alternative, you are likely a business that has either used Time Etc and wants something different, or is comparing VA services and weighing hourly blocks against a dedicated full-time placement. Time Etc is a long-established player with a strong reputation. This page is an honest comparison, not a takedown.

What Is Time Etc?

Time Etc is a UK-founded virtual assistant service that has been active for over 20 years according to their public website. They match clients with a VA and sell time in hourly blocks through a subscription model. Based on their public information, their VAs are primarily based in the UK and the US, and they serve both UK and US markets. Their strength is in VA, administrative, and operations work, and their model suits businesses that need a few hours of support per week rather than a full-time team member.

What Is Teckas?

Teckas is a managed remote staffing firm that places dedicated full-time professionals from India with US and Canadian businesses. Pricing is a flat all-inclusive monthly rate from $1,200 to $2,500, with no hourly tracking. Every placement is full-time, dedicated to a single client, and works a full shift aligned to US or Canadian time zones. We handle employment, HR, payroll, and retention.

Why People Search for Time Etc Alternatives

The most common reason buyers look at alternatives is role volume. Time Etc's hourly subscription model is elegant for a few hours a week, but the math shifts once you need 20, 30, or 40 hours. At that volume, a dedicated full-time placement often costs less per hour and gives you continuous context from one person instead of scheduling discrete tasks.

A second reason is role scope. Time Etc is strongest in VA, admin, and operations. Businesses that also need finance, engineering, analytics, or marketing specialists sometimes want a provider with a broader catalog.

What Time Etc Does Well

  • Established reputation: 20+ years in the VA category per their public website, with strong brand recognition.
  • UK and US talent: Native-English VAs from two major markets, well suited for client-facing work that benefits from local context.
  • Hourly flexibility: The hourly block model is ideal for businesses that need a small, variable amount of help each week.
  • Matching process: Clients are paired with a specific VA rather than getting rotating assistants.
  • Low commitment to start: Small monthly plans make it easy to test the service without a large spend.

What Teckas Does Differently

  • Full-time dedicated pro: Every Teckas placement works 40 hours a week exclusively for one client. Not hours, not tasks, a full team member.
  • Flat monthly pricing: $1,200 to $2,500 all-inclusive. No hour counting, no overage charges, no plan ladders.
  • India-based talent: Access to India's large pool of VA, finance, engineering, analytics, and operations professionals.
  • Broader roles: Beyond VA and admin, we staff developers, bookkeepers, analysts, ops managers, and more.
  • Managed employment: We employ the professional, handle HR and payroll, and step in on retention or performance issues.

When Time Etc Is the Better Choice

Time Etc is likely the better fit when:

  • You need a small, variable number of hours per week rather than a full-time person
  • You want a UK or US-based VA and native-market familiarity matters
  • Your tasks are primarily administrative, scheduling, inbox, or light operations work
  • You are starting small and want a low monthly floor
  • You are a UK business and want UK-hours coverage

When Teckas Is the Better Fit

Teckas tends to be the better fit when:

  • You need 30 to 40+ hours per week of consistent support
  • You want flat monthly pricing rather than hours-based billing
  • Your role extends beyond admin into bookkeeping, development, analytics, or operations
  • You want a dedicated person who builds long-term context, not task-by-task handoffs
  • You are a US or Canadian business looking for coverage in your time zone

Making the Decision

The short version: under 15 hours per week of admin work, Time Etc's model is elegant and often the cheaper path. Over 25 hours per week, or when the role spans multiple functions, a dedicated full-time placement usually delivers better economics and continuity. Some clients even run both: light admin via an hourly VA and dedicated full-time roles via Teckas.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Teckas cheaper than Time Etc?
They price differently. Time Etc publicly sells hourly blocks on a subscription model, which is very cost-effective for light, occasional use. Teckas prices a full-time dedicated professional at $1,200 to $2,500 per month all-inclusive. For 5 to 10 hours per week of VA work, Time Etc is often less expensive. For 30 to 40 hours per week, Teckas typically comes out lower per hour and includes HR, payroll, and management.
What is the main difference between Time Etc and Teckas?
Time Etc is a UK-founded VA service that sells hourly blocks and matches you with a VA, with VAs primarily based in the UK and US per their public website. Teckas is a managed remote staffing firm that places dedicated full-time India-based professionals at a flat monthly rate. Time Etc excels at part-time, hours-based VA support; Teckas is built for full-time dedicated roles.
Is the Teckas VA shared across clients?
No. Every Teckas placement works 40 hours per week exclusively for one client. There are no shared pools and no rotation across accounts.
Can Teckas work UK hours like Time Etc?
Teckas is focused on US and Canadian clients and staffs full shifts in those time zones. For UK-hours coverage from UK-based VAs, Time Etc is the better fit.
