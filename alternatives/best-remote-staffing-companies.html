Why remote staffing matters in 2026

A few years ago, remote staffing meant "virtual assistant." Today it spans almost every non-customer-facing role a North American business fills: software developers, finance analysts, operations managers, marketing specialists, executive assistants, customer support, sales development, and design. The reason is simple. Salaries for equivalent talent in India, the Philippines, and LatAm are a fraction of US or Canadian salaries, and the infrastructure for working together remotely has caught up. The question buyers now ask is not whether to hire remotely but who to partner with.

Our list focuses on companies that have been operating for more than three years, serve US and Canadian clients at meaningful scale, and publish enough information for buyers to make an informed decision. 1. Teckas

Region. India. Pricing. $1,200 to $2,500 per month all-inclusive. Roles. Software development, finance and accounting, operations, marketing, executive assistance, back-office analytics.

Teckas places dedicated full-time professionals from India with US and Canadian businesses under a managed model that includes employment, payroll, benefits, HR, and replacement. Month-to-month agreements. Best for teams hiring long-term roles where management support matters.

Honest limitation. India-only talent region. If you need Philippines voice agents or LatAm time zone coverage, another provider on this list is a better fit.

2. BruntWork

Region. Primarily Philippines. Pricing. Publicly listed hourly rates starting at approximately $4 to $10 per hour. Roles. Customer support, virtual assistants, sales support, back-office.

BruntWork is a large Philippines-focused outsourcing company with hourly billing. Known for high-volume operations and a broad agent catalog.

Honest limitation. Hourly billing creates tracking overhead, and the Philippines talent region does not match every role profile. Pool-based assignment options exist for some plans.

3. Wing Assistant

Region. Philippines and LatAm. Pricing. Publicly listed at approximately $599 to $2,400 per month. Roles. General VA, executive assistant, marketing assistant, sales assistant.

Wing Assistant is a polished subscription VA service with clear tiered pricing and a strong onboarding platform. Popular among startups.

Honest limitation. Entry tiers are part-time, and specialized technical or finance roles are outside the core catalog.

4. Support Shepherd

Region. Philippines and LatAm. Pricing. Flat monthly, publicly listed at approximately $1,800 to $2,500 per hire. Roles. Customer support, VA, back-office, sales support.

Support Shepherd is a US-based remote staffing firm that adds a US account management layer over offshore talent. Good middle ground for US teams new to offshore hiring.

Honest limitation. Not the cheapest option because of the US management overhead, and limited India talent.

5. BELAY

Region. United States. Pricing. Premium, quoted after discovery call. Roles. Executive assistant, bookkeeping, social media, website management.

BELAY matches US-based contractors to executives and small businesses. High-touch matching process and strong reputation among faith-based and SMB buyers.

Honest limitation. US talent means significantly higher cost than any offshore option on this list.

6. MyOutDesk

Region. Philippines. Pricing. Subscription from approximately $1,988 per month. Roles. Real estate VAs, inside sales agents, transaction coordinators, marketing.

MyOutDesk is a long-running remote staffing company with deep specialization in real estate and mortgage industries. Strong playbooks for that vertical.

Honest limitation. Vertical specialization is a strength for real estate and a weaker fit for other industries. Pricing is toward the higher end of offshore providers.

7. Prialto

Region. Philippines, Kenya, Guatemala. Pricing. Premium, publicly listed from approximately $1,700 per month. Roles. Executive assistants, sales operations, CRM hygiene, administrative support.

Prialto is an enterprise-focused managed assistant service with strong operational support layered behind each placement. Often chosen by larger sales organizations.

Honest limitation. Enterprise model and overhead make it more expensive than most offshore VA options. Not the right fit for individual founders.

8. Virtual Latinos

Region. LatAm (Mexico, Colombia, Argentina and others). Pricing. Placement fee plus salary you pay directly. Roles. VAs, marketing, operations, customer support.

Virtual Latinos is a direct-hire facilitation service that connects US businesses with vetted LatAm candidates. You employ the contractor directly after placement.

Honest limitation. No ongoing managed services after placement unless separately contracted. You handle payroll, compliance, and retention.

How to pick the right remote staffing company

  • Skilled full-time professional roles. Teckas for India talent, Prialto for enterprise scale.
  • High-volume customer support or inbound sales. BruntWork or Support Shepherd.
  • Flexible, tiered VA hours for a startup. Wing Assistant.
  • US-only talent. BELAY.
  • Industry-specialized VAs in real estate or mortgage. MyOutDesk.
  • LatAm time zone overlap. Virtual Latinos or Support Shepherd.

Frequently asked questions

What is the best remote staffing company in 2026?
There is no single best remote staffing company. The best choice depends on the roles you are filling, your preferred talent region, and whether you want a managed HR layer. For dedicated India-based professionals with managed HR, Teckas is a strong option. For Philippines-first outsourcing at hourly rates, BruntWork is well established. For LatAm time zone overlap, Support Shepherd or Virtual Latinos are common picks.
How much do remote staffing companies charge?
Offshore remote staffing companies typically charge between $1,200 and $2,500 per month all-inclusive for a dedicated full-time professional. Hourly pricing ranges from approximately $4 to $15 per hour for offshore talent. US-based remote staffing firms typically start at $3,500 per month and up.
What is the difference between remote staffing and a staffing agency?
A traditional staffing agency usually places workers on short contracts or as temp-to-perm placements, often billing hourly with a markup. Remote staffing companies, especially the managed model, place a dedicated professional long-term with a flat monthly fee that includes employment, payroll, benefits, and HR.
Is offshore remote staffing reliable for long-term roles?
Yes, when you pick a provider that operates with a dedicated, managed model rather than a shared pool. Firms like Teckas, Prialto, and MyOutDesk are built for long-term placements. Turnover is comparable to local hiring when the provider runs a real HR and retention layer.
Which talent region is best?
India has the deepest professional skill pool for engineering, finance, analytics, and operations. The Philippines is strongest for voice customer support and general VA roles. LatAm offers US time zone overlap, useful for roles requiring real-time collaboration. The right region depends on the role.
