BruntWork Alternative: Why Businesses Choose Teckas for Remote Staffing

Evaluating BruntWork for remote staff? Here is an honest, side-by-side look at how Teckas compares on pricing, talent region, management model, and when each one is the better choice.

If you are searching for a BruntWork alternative, you are likely a US or Canadian business that has either used BruntWork and wants something different, or is weighing offshore staffing options and comparing providers. Both BruntWork and Teckas help companies hire remote team members at lower costs than local hires. Both are legitimate options. But the two operate with meaningfully different models, talent regions, and pricing structures. This guide is a factual, respectful comparison so you can choose the right fit.

What Is BruntWork?

BruntWork is an outsourcing and remote staffing company that sources talent primarily from the Philippines. Based on their publicly available information, BruntWork offers agents across a wide range of categories, with a strong focus on customer support, virtual assistants, sales development, and other back-office roles. Their pricing is published in an hourly format, with rates that publicly suggest a starting range of roughly $4 to $10 per hour depending on role seniority and specialization. They serve a broad international client base and are well known within the offshore outsourcing category.

What Is Teckas?

Teckas is a managed remote staffing firm that places dedicated full-time professionals from India with US and Canadian businesses. Our model is closer to an "employer-of-record plus recruiting plus HR" hybrid: we source, vet, and employ the professional, while you direct their day-to-day work. Pricing is a flat all-inclusive monthly rate ranging from $1,200 to $2,500 per role depending on seniority and function, with no hourly tracking, no minimum commitment beyond month-to-month, and a replacement guarantee included.

Teckas is focused on dedicated, full-time, long-term placements rather than transactional hourly work. Common roles we staff include software developers, finance and accounting professionals, operations managers, marketing specialists, executive assistants, and back-office analysts.

Why People Look for BruntWork Alternatives

From conversations with prospects who have previously used BruntWork or similar Philippines-based outsourcing companies, the most common reasons for exploring alternatives are:

  • A preference for India-based talent, particularly for engineering, finance, analytics, and technical operations
  • Wanting a flat monthly all-inclusive price instead of hourly billing
  • Looking for a more dedicated, integrated team member rather than a transactional agent
  • Needing a managed HR layer (performance support, retention) rather than a pure staffing handoff
  • Coverage across a specific US or Canadian time zone with shift flexibility

None of these are criticisms of BruntWork. They simply reflect different buying preferences.

What BruntWork Does Well

Before drawing contrasts, it is worth noting where BruntWork is genuinely strong. Based on their public information and market reputation:

  • Philippines talent pool: The Philippines has a deep bench of customer-facing agents with neutral, conversational English well suited to US-consumer-facing roles like inbound customer support, phone sales, and retail CS.
  • Hourly flexibility: BruntWork's hourly pricing structure suits companies that need part-time coverage or have seasonal volume spikes.
  • Broad role catalog: They publicly advertise a wide variety of agent types, useful for companies trying different functions without long commitments.
  • High-volume operations: For companies needing many agents quickly for support or sales functions, BruntWork's scale is a genuine strength.

Where Teckas Is Different

Teckas is not trying to be BruntWork. The model is intentionally different:

  • India-based talent: Our entire recruiting and employment operation is in India. This matters if you need deep technical, financial, or analytical roles where India has one of the largest trained-professional pools globally.
  • Dedicated, full-time: Every Teckas placement works 40 hours a week exclusively for one client. No shared pools, no shift rotations with other accounts.
  • Flat monthly pricing: A single all-inclusive monthly rate covering salary, benefits, HR, payroll, equipment allowance, and management. No hourly ticking.
  • Managed HR layer: We handle performance check-ins, retention conversations, and step in if a role is not working. This is part of the service, not an add-on.
  • US and Canadian focus: Contracts, time zones, communication norms, and compliance practices are built for North American clients.
  • Month-to-month: No long-term contracts, no minimum hour commitments, and a replacement guarantee if a fit is off.

Pricing Comparison

A direct hourly vs monthly comparison requires normalizing to the same unit. Assuming a standard 160-hour work month:

  • BruntWork: At a publicly referenced $5 to $10 per hour on a full-time equivalent basis, a dedicated agent would cost roughly $800 to $1,600 per month. Specialist or senior roles may be higher.
  • Teckas: $1,200 to $2,500 per month all-inclusive for a dedicated professional working 40 hours per week, which normalizes to roughly $7.50 to $15.60 per hour, including HR, payroll, benefits, equipment allowance, and managed support.

For pure hourly low-touch work, BruntWork may be less expensive. For dedicated full-time professionals in skilled roles where you want management included, Teckas's pricing is often comparable or favorable once you factor the inclusions.

When BruntWork Is the Better Choice

BruntWork is likely the better fit when:

  • You specifically want Philippines-based agents, especially for voice customer support where neutral conversational English is critical
  • You need hourly or part-time flexibility rather than full-time dedicated staff
  • You are scaling a high-volume customer support or inbound sales function fast
  • Your budget target is at the lowest end of the offshore staffing range and you are comfortable with a more transactional relationship
  • You already have internal HR, performance, and retention systems and do not need a managed layer

When Teckas Is the Better Fit

Teckas tends to be the better fit when:

  • You want India-based talent, especially for software engineering, finance, accounting, analytics, or operations
  • You want dedicated full-time professionals who integrate with your team long-term
  • You prefer predictable flat monthly pricing over hourly billing
  • You want a managed HR layer that handles performance and retention
  • You are a US or Canadian business and want a partner whose operations are built around your jurisdiction
  • You value a month-to-month commitment with a replacement guarantee

Making the Decision

Neither company is universally better. The right answer depends on your function, volume, budget, and preferred working model. If you are hiring ten customer support agents for a high-volume ecommerce operation, BruntWork's model may be a cleaner fit. If you are adding a senior developer, a financial analyst, or a dedicated operations manager who will become a long-term part of your team, Teckas is likely the stronger option.

You can also use both. Some of our clients run Philippines-based customer support through one provider while placing their dedicated engineering and back-office roles through Teckas.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Teckas cheaper than BruntWork?
The two companies price differently. BruntWork's publicly listed hourly rates start in the $4 to $10 per hour range depending on role, which can come out lower on a pure hourly basis for high-volume needs. Teckas prices monthly, all-inclusive, at $1,200 to $2,500 per dedicated professional working 40 hours a week, which typically works out to $7.50 to $15 per hour with HR, payroll, benefits, and management included. For full-time dedicated roles, the total cost is often comparable; for part-time or transactional work, BruntWork may be less expensive.
What is the main difference between BruntWork and Teckas?
BruntWork is a Philippines-focused outsourcing company that offers a wide range of agents at hourly rates. Teckas is a managed remote staffing firm that places dedicated professionals from India with US and Canadian companies at a flat monthly rate, with HR, payroll, and replacement included. The models overlap but target different needs: BruntWork is often a good fit for transactional, high-volume customer support; Teckas is built for dedicated full-time roles that integrate deeply with your team.
Does Teckas hire from the Philippines?
No. Teckas exclusively sources and employs talent from India. If your preference is Philippines-based agents, BruntWork is the better fit. If you want India-based professionals, especially for engineering, finance, analytics, and back-office operations, Teckas is focused there.
Can Teckas cover US time zones?
Yes. Teckas professionals work full shifts aligned to US Eastern, Central, Mountain, or Pacific time, or Canadian time zones, based on your preference. This is a standard part of our service.
