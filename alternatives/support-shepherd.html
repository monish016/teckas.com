Support Shepherd Alternative: Managed Remote Staffing with Teckas

Both Support Shepherd and Teckas are legitimate managed remote staffing partners. Here is an honest side-by-side look at how they compare on talent region, pricing, role variety, and which one fits different teams best.

If you are evaluating Support Shepherd and looking at alternatives to Support Shepherd, you are already on the right track. Both Support Shepherd and Teckas use the same core model: we find the person, employ them legally in their home country, handle HR and payroll, and place them as a dedicated full-time member of your team at a transparent monthly price. The differences are in geography, role focus, and a few operational details. This comparison is meant to be informative and neutral, not promotional.

What Is Support Shepherd?

Support Shepherd is a US-based managed remote staffing company that places full-time remote employees with US businesses. Based on their publicly available website information, Support Shepherd sources primarily from the Philippines and Latin America, focuses on a defined set of roles including virtual assistants, customer service representatives, operations staff, and similar support functions, and charges flat monthly pricing per hire. They are a transparent, well-regarded company in the managed staffing category.

What Is Teckas?

Teckas is a managed remote staffing firm based in India that places dedicated full-time professionals from India with US and Canadian businesses. Like Support Shepherd, our pricing is flat, monthly, and all-inclusive of HR, payroll, benefits, and management. Our pricing is $1,200 to $2,500 per month depending on role seniority, we work month-to-month with no minimum commitment, and we include a replacement guarantee.

Where we differ from Support Shepherd is in the geographic source of talent (India, not Philippines or LatAm) and in the breadth of roles we cover. Teckas regularly places software developers, finance and accounting professionals, data analysts, and technical operations staff, in addition to VAs and CSRs.

Why People Look for Support Shepherd Alternatives

Most buyers comparing Support Shepherd vs Teckas are doing honest vendor due diligence rather than reacting to a problem. Common reasons we see:

  • A preference for or requirement of India-based talent (common for technical and finance roles)
  • Need for roles outside Support Shepherd's core categories (especially developers, engineers, or senior finance)
  • Wanting multiple quotes for the same role from comparable providers
  • Specific time zone or shift arrangement requirements
  • Evaluating Canada-focused options in addition to US

What Support Shepherd Does Well

Support Shepherd is a respected player in the managed staffing category and brings several genuine strengths:

  • Philippines and Latin America talent: Strong cultural fit with US consumer-facing roles, time zone overlap with US business hours (especially for LatAm hires), and a deep pool of VAs and CSRs.
  • Transparent flat pricing: Like Teckas, they do not hide behind hourly billing or per-minute tracking.
  • Focused role catalog: Specialization in VAs, CSRs, and operations means deep matching expertise for those functions.
  • Established US presence: Their team is US-based which some buyers prefer for contracting, communication, and account management.

Where Teckas Is Different

Teckas is operationally similar, with a few meaningful differences:

  • India talent pool: India has one of the largest trained professional workforces globally, particularly strong in engineering, technology, finance, and analytics.
  • Broader role catalog: Beyond VAs and CSRs, we place developers, accountants, analysts, designers, marketing specialists, and operations managers.
  • Full US or Canada shift alignment: Our professionals work standard 40-hour shifts aligned to your local business hours, typically starting their workday in the evening India time.
  • Replacement guarantee included: If a match does not work out, we replace the professional at no additional cost.
  • Pricing at $1,200 to $2,500 per month: All-inclusive of salary, benefits, HR, payroll, equipment allowance, and managed support.

When Support Shepherd Is the Better Choice

Support Shepherd is likely the better fit when:

  • You prefer Philippines or Latin America talent for cultural or language reasons
  • You are hiring primarily for VA, CSR, or operations admin roles
  • You specifically want LatAm time zone alignment (e.g., real-time overlap with Pacific or Eastern business hours)
  • You want a US-based account team and prefer the US entity contracting relationship
  • Spanish-language capability is relevant to your role

When Teckas Is the Better Fit

Teckas tends to be the better fit when:

  • You want India-based talent, especially for software engineering, finance and accounting, or analytics roles
  • You need technical roles that Support Shepherd does not specialize in
  • You want a single all-inclusive monthly price with HR, payroll, benefits, and replacement included
  • You want US or Canadian time zone coverage and are fine with India-origin talent working that shift
  • You prefer month-to-month commitment flexibility
  • You are specifically a Canadian business looking for a provider with explicit Canadian focus

How the Hiring Process Compares

At a high level, both companies follow the same pattern: scoping call, role briefing, candidate shortlist, client interviews, and onboarding. Differences in timeline and process are small and generally a matter of scheduling. Teckas typically presents a shortlist within 7 to 10 business days of the initial scoping call, and onboards within 2 to 3 weeks total. Support Shepherd publishes timelines on their site.

Either way, the real work is the role briefing, not the mechanics. A good scoping call, clear scorecards for what "good" looks like, and a consistent interview loop will determine fit more than which provider you choose.

Making the Decision

Support Shepherd and Teckas are both legitimate, transparent, managed staffing providers. The best way to choose is to run the same role through both providers, compare candidates, and see which team produces a stronger shortlist for your specific need. If the role is a VA or CSR with LatAm time zone preference, Support Shepherd is often a natural fit. If the role is a developer, financial analyst, or technical operations manager, Teckas tends to have the deeper pool.
