What a Follow Up Boss Virtual Assistant Does

A Follow Up Boss virtual assistant is a remote team member who lives inside your FUB account every day, routing new leads the moment they hit the CRM, enrolling them in the right action plan, nudging cold leads with drip campaigns, keeping smart lists clean, and making sure no agent-assigned lead slips through the cracks. For real estate teams running lead-gen spend across Zillow, Realtor.com, Google, and Facebook, the speed-to-lead difference between a trained FUB VA and nobody watching is the difference between a booked appointment and a dead lead.

Every Teckas FUB VA is tested on real Follow Up Boss tasks before you ever see their resume. They know the shape of the platform: the inbox, the pond, the smart lists, the action plan builder, and the team @mention flow. You are not paying someone to read the FUB help docs for the first two weeks.
