What a Gorgias Customer Support Agent Does

A Gorgias customer support agent is a dedicated remote CX teammate who lives inside your Gorgias helpdesk, replying to email, live chat, Instagram and Facebook DMs, and SMS tickets as they land. For a DTC Shopify brand, this is the single most customer-visible role on your team. Every reply shapes LTV, review sentiment, and refund rate. Hiring a skilled Gorgias agent is not the same as hiring a generic VA, because the difference between a trained Gorgias agent and an untrained one is measured in first-response time, macro accuracy, and how many tickets auto-resolve without touching a human.

The agents Teckas places already know the shape of Gorgias: they use keyboard shortcuts, open the Shopify sidebar from the ticket, apply the right macro for the intent, tag for reporting, and escalate anything that needs a human second set of eyes. You are not paying someone to learn your helpdesk.

Core Workflows Your Gorgias Agent Owns

  • Answering email, live chat, SMS, and social DM tickets inside Gorgias
  • Using macros and auto-responses for shipping updates, returns, and FAQ questions
  • Looking up order status, tracking, and fulfillment directly from the Shopify sidebar
  • Issuing refunds, store credit, replacements, and cancellations per your policy
  • Handling WISMO (where is my order) tickets at scale
  • Processing returns through Loop, AfterShip Returns, or Happy Returns
  • Escalating damaged-on-arrival, lost package, and chargeback cases to a senior agent
  • Tagging tickets for reporting and VOC analysis
  • Maintaining the macro library as product, shipping, and policy details change
  • Running CSAT surveys and flagging detractors for proactive outreach
  • Pulling weekly volume, first-response, and resolution-time reports for the CX manager

Gorgias-Specific Skills They Bring

We place agents who have hands-on, production-level time inside Gorgias, not agents who just list it on a resume. Expect fluency in:

  • Macros and variables including dynamic variables, conditional macros, and multi-step macros
  • Rules and triage for auto-tagging, auto-responding, and routing by intent and channel
  • Shopify integration for refunds, order edits, cancellation, and LTV lookup from the sidebar
  • Intent detection and AI autoresponders where enabled
  • Multi-store and multi-brand configurations if you run more than one Shopify store
  • Views and assignments so tickets land with the right agent on the right shift
  • Gorgias Statistics and reporting for CSAT, first response time, and resolution time
  • Self-service and Help Center configuration for deflection
  • Integrations with Recharge, Loop, ShipBob, Yotpo, Klaviyo, and Postscript

Related Tools They Can Work With

A DTC CX seat never lives in one tool. Our Gorgias agents are comfortable inside the wider Shopify stack:

  • Shopify and Shopify Plus for order lookup, refunds, and store credit
  • Recharge, Bold, and Skio for subscription cancellation and swap workflows
  • Loop Returns, AfterShip Returns, and ReturnLogic for returns triage
  • ShipStation, ShipBob, and ShipHero for fulfillment status and address changes
  • Klaviyo, Postscript, and Attentive for list hygiene and unsubscribe handling
  • Yotpo, Okendo, and Judge.me for review moderation
  • Slack, Notion, and Loom for internal collaboration
