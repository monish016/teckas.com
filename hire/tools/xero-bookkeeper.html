What a Xero Bookkeeper for Hire Does

A Xero bookkeeper for hire is a full-time remote team member who lives inside your Xero organization every day. They reconcile bank feeds in real time using Xero's rule-based match, process bills through Hubdoc and Xero's AP automation, run payroll journals, reconcile Stripe and PayPal clearing accounts, and produce a clean set of management accounts at month end. For a growing business already on Xero, the alternative is either hiring a local bookkeeper at roughly four times the cost, or cobbling together a freelance patchwork that falls behind the first time anything unusual lands in the ledger.

Teckas places Xero-certified bookkeepers who already know the Xero way: light chart of accounts, heavy use of tracking categories, aggressive automation of the bank feed, and tight AP workflow. You are not paying someone to learn Xero. You are paying someone who has been closing Xero files for years.

Core Workflows Your Xero Bookkeeper Runs

  • Daily bank feed reconciliation using Xero rules, cash coding, and the find and match screen
  • Accounts payable through Xero's bills workflow, Hubdoc, Dext, or AutoEntry bill capture
  • Accounts receivable with Xero invoices, repeating invoices, and Stripe or GoCardless payments
  • Payroll journal import from Gusto, ADP, or Xero Payroll where available
  • Multi-currency reconciliation and FX revaluation inside Xero
  • Tracking categories for departments, locations, projects, or properties
  • A2X reconciliation for Shopify, Amazon, Etsy, eBay, and Walmart marketplaces
  • Sales tax and GST/HST/PST tracking with filing-ready reports
  • Fixed asset register maintenance and monthly depreciation
  • Month-end close: accruals, prepayments, deferred revenue, and adjusting entries
  • Management reporting packs with P&L, balance sheet, cash summary, and aged receivables
