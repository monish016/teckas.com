What a Virtual Dispatcher for HVAC Actually Does

A virtual dispatcher for HVAC is a remote team member who answers your inbound calls, books service appointments, schedules and reroutes technicians, updates your field service software in real time, and keeps customers informed from the moment they call to the moment the truck pulls in. They sit in your dispatch software the same way a local dispatcher would, just without the local payroll overhead.

For HVAC, plumbing, and electrical companies running 3 to 30 trucks, a dedicated remote dispatcher is often the single highest-leverage hire. Missed calls equal missed revenue, and according to industry surveys from ServiceTitan and Housecall Pro, many home service companies miss 20 to 35 percent of inbound calls during peak season. An always-on virtual HVAC dispatcher closes that gap.

Key Responsibilities of a Remote HVAC Dispatcher

  • Answer inbound service calls with a professional, on-brand greeting
  • Book new appointments and route them to the right technician by zip code, skill, and availability
  • Handle dispatch board updates in ServiceTitan, Housecall Pro, Jobber, FieldEdge, or ServiceFusion
  • Confirm appointments the day before and the morning of the visit
  • Reschedule and reroute techs when jobs run over or cancellations hit
  • Manage overflow calls during summer cooling and winter heating peaks
  • Coordinate maintenance agreement renewals and seasonal tune-up campaigns
  • Update customer records, job notes, and tags in the CRM
  • Escalate emergency no-heat and no-cool calls per your SLA
  • Chase invoices, collect outstanding balances, and flag collections issues
  • Pull daily and weekly dispatch reports for the owner or operations manager
