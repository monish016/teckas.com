A managed remote bookkeeper for small business solves that. Teckas provides outsourced bookkeeper USA and Canada services as full-time remote staff, not packaged hour bundles. Your bookkeeper learns your business, your vendors, your chart of accounts, and your month-end rhythm, the same way an in-house hire would.

Key Responsibilities of a Remote Bookkeeper

  • Daily transaction categorization in QuickBooks Online, Xero, or equivalent
  • Bank, credit card, and merchant account reconciliations
  • Accounts receivable (AR) invoicing, collections, and aging reports
  • Accounts payable (AP) bill entry, approval routing, and payment runs
  • Month-end close checklist and journal entries
  • Expense categorization and receipt capture via Dext, Hubdoc, or Ramp
  • Payroll processing support in Gusto, ADP, QuickBooks Payroll, or Wagepoint
  • Sales tax, GST, HST, and PST tracking and filing prep
  • Monthly P&L, balance sheet, and cash flow reports for the owner
  • Year-end tax prep package for your CPA or tax preparer
  • 1099 and T4A tracking and issuance at year-end
  • Budget versus actual tracking and basic financial dashboards
