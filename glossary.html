Glossary

Remote Staffing Glossary

Plain-English definitions of 50 terms that come up when hiring remote staff. No jargon loops. Source-cited where it matters.

Staffing Models

Staffing and Hiring Models

Employer of Record (EOR)

Third-party company that legally employs workers on behalf of another business.

BPO

Business Process Outsourcing. Delegating specific business processes to an external provider.

Managed Staffing

Hybrid model combining staffing agency and EOR services.

Staff Augmentation

Temporarily adding external workers to your existing team.

RPO

Recruitment Process Outsourcing. Outsourcing recruitment to a third party.

PEO

Professional Employer Organization. Co-employment model for HR and benefits.

Offshore vs Nearshore vs Onshore

Three geographic staffing models compared.

W2 vs 1099

The difference between employee and contractor classifications in the US.

Staffing Agency

Company that matches employers with job candidates.

Outsourcing

Contracting business functions to an external provider.

Co-Employment

Two employers sharing legal responsibility for the same employee.

Remote Staffing

Hiring professionals who work remotely, often across borders.

Virtual Staffing

Staffing professionals who work entirely online.

IT Staffing

Staffing specifically for technology and software roles.

Professional Staffing

Staffing for skilled roles requiring specialized education or training.
Roles & Titles

Job Titles Explained

Virtual Assistant (VA)

Remote administrative professional supporting executives or businesses.

Executive Assistant

Senior-level assistant supporting C-suite or senior leadership.

SDR

Sales Development Representative. Outbound lead qualification role.

BDR

Business Development Representative. Focused on new business development.

Inside Sales Agent (ISA)

Sales professional working remote or office-based, often in real estate.

Transaction Coordinator

Real estate role managing contract-to-close paperwork and compliance.

Virtual Receptionist

Remote professional handling inbound calls and customer communications.

Bookkeeper vs Accountant

The difference between daily recordkeeping and strategic financial analysis.

Medical Biller

Healthcare admin role handling insurance claims and revenue cycle.

Dispatcher

Coordinates service appointments, technician routing, and customer communication.

Customer Success Manager

Post-sale role ensuring customers achieve their desired outcomes.

Account Coordinator

Agency or client-services role managing day-to-day account operations.

Paralegal

Legal support professional working under attorney supervision.

QA Engineer

Quality assurance professional who tests software before release.

DevOps Engineer

Bridges software development and IT operations for reliable deployments.
Business Processes

Business and Operations Terms

SLA

Service-Level Agreement. Documented standards a vendor commits to.

KPI

Key Performance Indicator. Measurable value of business performance.

OKR

Objectives and Key Results. Goal-setting framework.

Payroll Processing

The full lifecycle of calculating, distributing, and reporting employee pay.

Time Tracking

Recording hours worked for billing, payroll, or productivity.

Cost Per Hire

Total cost to fill one position, including recruitment and overhead.

Time to Hire

Days from opening a role to the new hire accepting an offer.

Employee Retention

The ability to keep employees from leaving.

Offshoring vs Outsourcing

Two related but distinct business models compared.

Back-Office Operations

Internal business functions not customer-facing.
Compliance & Legal

Compliance and Legal Terms

DPDP Act (India)

India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023.

FEMA (Remote Payments)

India's Foreign Exchange Management Act governing cross-border payments.

GDPR for Offshore Staff

EU data protection implications when staff are outside the EU.

HIPAA for Remote Medical Billing

Privacy rules for remote healthcare admin workers.

NDA

Non-Disclosure Agreement. Contract protecting confidential information.
Tools & Tech

Tools and Technology Terms

CRM

Customer Relationship Management software.

ATS

Applicant Tracking System. Software for managing hiring pipelines.

HRIS

Human Resources Information System.

Time Zone Management

Practices for coordinating teams across different time zones.

Remote Collaboration Stack

The set of tools a remote team uses daily (Slack, Zoom, Notion, etc.).
