Why BC Founders Hire Remote VAs

British Columbia has a deep base of founder-led businesses: Vancouver's tech and film/VFX cluster, Victoria's software and government professional services, and a wide SMB base across tourism, real estate, and natural resources. Vancouver executive assistant pay per Glassdoor runs CAD $65,000 to $95,000 per year, with senior EAs at Vancouver tech companies clearing CAD $110,000. Loaded with CPP, EI, EHT, extended health, and statutory vacation, a single seat runs CAD $7,500 to $11,000 per month. For a founder at a 10-person company, that cost is disproportionate to the work.

A Teckas VA works Pacific Time overlap with your Vancouver schedule, handles calendar, inbox, travel, expenses, research, and light CRM. BLS-equivalent Canadian data (Statistics Canada NOC 13110 executive assistants) tracks around CAD $62,000 median.
