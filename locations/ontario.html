Why Ontario Businesses Are Going Remote

Ontario is the largest provincial economy in Canada and home to Toronto, the country's financial services capital (TSX, Big Five banks, insurance). The Toronto-Waterloo corridor is one of North America's densest tech clusters, anchored by Shopify, OpenText, Thomson Reuters, and a deep Waterloo engineering ecosystem. Ontario tech compensation has climbed sharply since 2020, with Toronto mid-level engineers quoted between CAD 130,000 and CAD 180,000 per Levels.fyi and Indeed Canada data. Compared to the pre-2020 baseline, that is a 30 to 40 percent increase on loaded cost, and CPP / EI / EHT contributions plus Ontario health tax add meaningfully on top.

Ontario employers are also dealing with a housing-driven cost-of-living shock in the GTA and Ottawa, which has pushed up baseline salary expectations even for operational roles. Teckas is used by Ontario fintechs, SaaS operators, property management groups, and professional services firms to staff the non-strategic layer of their team from India while staying on Toronto Eastern Time.

How Working in Ontario Eastern Time Works

Ontario runs on Eastern Time (same as Toronto and New York). India is 9.5 to 10.5 hours ahead. A 9am to 6pm ET shift is 6:30pm to 3:30am IST, which we staff as a dedicated full-time shift.

Compliance and Employment Notes

Teckas staff are employees of Teckas Technologies Pvt. Ltd. in India, not Ontario employees or Canadian contractors. You are engaging us for services under a commercial agreement, not hiring a Canadian worker, so Employment Standards Act (ESA), CPP / EI, WSIB, and Ontario EHT do not apply to the engagement. For financial services clients we can sign NDAs, DPAs, PIPEDA-aligned data handling commitments, and OSFI-relevant data residency addenda on request. Invoicing is standard USD; Canadian clients do not remit US or Indian withholding on Teckas invoices (international service provider).
