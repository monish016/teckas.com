Why New York Brands and Agencies Hire Remote Marketers

New York is the largest advertising and media market in North America. Madison Avenue still houses a huge share of global holding-company agencies (WPP, Omnicom, Publicis, IPG), the Flatiron and SoHo tech-agency belt covers hundreds of independent shops, and NYC-based brands from Glossier to Peloton to Spotify are headquartered in Manhattan and Brooklyn. Demand for execution-level paid media, SEO, and lifecycle marketers is constant, and the supply is expensive.

Built In NYC regularly reports mid-level marketing specialist pay at $75,000 to $95,000, and senior paid social managers commonly clear $110,000. Loaded with NY state income tax, NYC and Yonkers local tax, healthcare, 401k, and benefits, a single seat costs $7,500 to $10,000 per month. For an agency billing mid-five-figure monthly retainers across 10 clients, that overhead is the difference between healthy margin and break-even.
