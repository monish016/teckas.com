Why New York DTC Brands Hire Remote Ecommerce CS

New York City is the center of gravity for the US DTC industry. Brooklyn and Manhattan host Warby Parker, Glossier, Casper, Harry's, Allbirds, Bonobos, Mejuri, and hundreds of smaller Shopify-based brands. Retail Dive and Modern Retail regularly cover the NYC DTC founder density, and most brands hit their first "we need 24/5 support" wall between $5M and $20M in annual GMV. At that point, the choice is usually between hiring another local NYC agent at $55,000 to $65,000 per year or building a remote support team.

Local NYC hiring is not cheap. Glassdoor puts Manhattan and Brooklyn CSR pay at $48,000 to $62,000, and loaded with benefits and NY state and city income tax gross-up expectations, the real cost runs $5,000 to $6,500 per month per seat. New York also has specific labor laws (NYC paid sick leave, statewide pay-transparency disclosure on job postings, frequency-of-pay rules) that add compliance overhead. For a 3-person DTC brand at $8M in GMV, that overhead is disproportionate.

A Teckas remote agent is a Teckas India employee working your Eastern Time shift, inside your Shopify and Gorgias, handling your brand tone. BLS May 2023 lists national CSR median pay at $39,680 with NY metros trending 20 to 35 percent above.
