Why Los Angeles Businesses Hire Remote

Los Angeles has the highest concentration of small businesses of any US metro - over 650,000 SMBs across LA County - and some of the highest loaded payroll costs in the country. Between California's minimum wage floors, state income tax, SDI, ETT, workers' comp, and the city of LA's own wage ordinances, a $50,000 base hire commonly lands north of $72,000-$78,000 loaded. That math is crushing for DTC brands, Silicon Beach post-seed startups, entertainment-industry service shops, and the long tail of SMBs in Koreatown, Sherman Oaks, and the South Bay.

The LA ecommerce ecosystem is particularly exposed. DTC founders running Shopify or Amazon brands are stacking tools (Klaviyo, Gorgias, Recharge, Triple Whale) that require dedicated operators, but cannot justify hiring three $65K+ specialists. LA creator-economy businesses, film-adjacent rental houses, and Silicon Beach startups are in the same squeeze. Los Angeles remote staffing through Teckas gives them trained, full-time team members working Pacific Time at a fraction of the loaded LA cost.

And because Teckas staff are Indian-domiciled employees of Teckas Technologies Pvt. Ltd., not California-resident 1099 contractors, the arrangement sidesteps AB5 classification concerns entirely.

How LA's Time Zone Works With India

Los Angeles runs on Pacific Time. India Standard Time is 12 hours 30 minutes ahead of PT during standard time and 12 hours 30 minutes during daylight saving. A 9am to 6pm PT LA workday lands at 9:30pm to 6:30am IST in India. Our LA-dedicated staff are hired for that night shift as their primary, full-time schedule. You get same-day replies during your LA standup, not next-morning catchup.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does California AB5 affect hiring Teckas remote staff?

Yes. Our staff work your full LA business day, typically 9am to 6pm PT. That maps to 9:30pm to 6:30am IST in India, and our team is hired specifically for that shift as their full-time hours.
