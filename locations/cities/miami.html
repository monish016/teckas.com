Why Miami Businesses Hire Remote

Miami has become the US's fastest-repositioning financial city. Citadel moved its global headquarters to Brickell. Ken Griffin, SoftBank, Blackstone, and a cascade of New York and San Francisco hedge funds, family offices, and crypto firms opened Miami offices between 2021 and 2024. That NY-to-Miami migration has been a gift to Florida's economy, but it has also compressed the local hiring market - operations, finance, compliance, and admin talent in Miami now command salaries within 15 to 25 percent of Manhattan ranges per Indeed and Glassdoor South Florida data.

At the same time, Miami is the US gateway to Latin America. Major LatAm-headquartered firms run their North American back offices in Coral Gables and Doral. Miami-Dade's real estate market, especially in Brickell, Edgewater, and Coconut Grove, is running at extraordinary transaction volume. And the Miami creator and DTC ecosystem is growing quickly. All of these businesses share the same problem: operating costs climbing faster than revenue, and difficulty hiring ops headcount without burning capital.

Miami remote staffing through Teckas gives you trained, full-time team members in Eastern Time - right alongside your Brickell team - at one-third to one-fourth of local loaded cost.

How Miami's Time Zone Works With India

Miami runs on Eastern Time. India Standard Time is 9 hours 30 minutes ahead of ET during standard time and 9 hours 30 minutes during daylight saving. A Miami 9am to 6pm ET workday lands at 6:30pm to 3:30am IST. Our Miami-dedicated staff work that window as their full-time shift. You get Slack replies and phone coverage during your Brickell business hours, not at end-of-day India time.
