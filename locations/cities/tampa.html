Why Tampa Businesses Hire Remote

Tampa Bay has quietly become one of the fastest-growing metros in the US South. Raymond James, Jabil, MetLife Global Technology Center, and Amgen anchor the corporate base. The Tampa-St. Pete-Clearwater MSA hosts a dense cluster of financial services firms, RIAs, and insurance carriers. Healthcare is a major employer thanks to BayCare, AdventHealth, and the Moffitt Cancer Center. And a small but growing tech scene is emerging around SaaS, cybersecurity, and healthtech firms in Water Street and St. Petersburg's downtown innovation district.

The challenge for Tampa Bay SMBs is that this growth has tightened the labor market meaningfully. Tampa is not New York or San Francisco, but a competent operations associate, bookkeeper, or medical biller who would have cost $50,000 loaded in 2019 now clears $60,000-$68,000 loaded per Indeed and BLS Tampa MSA data. For small business owners running 5 to 20 person shops, that compression eats margin, and most of them are already wearing too many operational hats themselves.

Tampa remote staffing through Teckas gives those owners trained, full-time team members in Eastern Time - usually one or two seats to start - at one-third of the loaded local cost.

How Tampa's Time Zone Works With India

Tampa runs on Eastern Time. India Standard Time is 9 hours 30 minutes ahead of ET. A Tampa 8am to 5pm ET workday maps to 5:30pm to 2:30am IST. Our staff work that window as their full-time shift, so you get real-time Slack coverage and phone response during your Tampa business day.
