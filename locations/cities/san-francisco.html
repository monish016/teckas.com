Why San Francisco Businesses Hire Remote

San Francisco remains the single most expensive tech labor market in the world. Per Levels.fyi, Glassdoor, and Built In SF 2024-2025 data, a mid-level software engineer in SF averages roughly $160,000-$200,000 base before equity, and that ladder climbs rapidly at senior and staff. Even non-engineering roles - customer success managers, product marketers, ops leads - routinely clear $120,000-$150,000 loaded. For a post-Series A startup trying to make 18 months of runway last 24, that math is brutal.

SF also operates under a dominant hybrid-remote culture. Since 2020, SF tech has normalized distributed work in a way most other cities have not, which makes plugging in remote Teckas team members culturally seamless. Our clients in SoMa, Mission Bay, and Palo Alto typically keep their senior architects, PMs, and design leads local and then build out the pod - frontend, backend, QA, CS, and data - through Teckas at one-fourth to one-fifth of the local loaded rate.

Because Teckas staff are employees of our Indian entity, there is no California payroll nexus, no AB5 exposure, and no 1099 classification risk. You pay a simple monthly fee and we handle payroll, benefits, equipment, and HR in India.

Top Roles SF Companies Outsource

  • Software engineers. Frontend (React, Vue), backend (Node, Python, Go, Ruby), and full-stack for SF SaaS, devtools, and AI startups.

  • RevOps and sales ops. Salesforce and HubSpot admins, pipeline hygiene, and lead-routing for SF sales orgs.

How SF's Time Zone Works With India

San Francisco is on Pacific Time. India Standard Time is 12 hours 30 minutes ahead. An SF 9am to 6pm PT workday is 9:30pm to 6:30am IST in India. Our engineers and CS reps are hired for that window as their full-time shift. They are online for your SF standup, your pull-request reviews, and your Slack pings in real time - no 1-hour overlap tricks.
