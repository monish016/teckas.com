Why Columbus Businesses Hire Remote

Columbus has been one of the quietest success stories in the Midwest. Nationwide Mutual Insurance, Huntington Bancshares, L Brands (Victoria's Secret and Bath & Body Works), American Electric Power, and Cardinal Health anchor the corporate base. Rickenbacker International Airport and the Central Ohio distribution corridor have made Columbus a logistics hub for Amazon, FedEx, and DHL. Intel's $20 billion semiconductor fabrication project in Licking County is about to transform the regional labor market further. The Ohio State University generates a steady flow of graduates, and the city's population has grown roughly 15 percent since 2010.

For Columbus SMBs - insurance brokerages, RIAs, logistics operators, small manufacturers, service businesses, and a growing tech scene - the problem is that growth has outpaced admin capacity. Owners are stretched across sales, operations, finance, HR, and marketing simultaneously, often because adding a $55,000 loaded hire is painful at current scale. Columbus remote staffing through Teckas gives those owners a trained, full-time team member in Eastern Time at one-third of loaded local cost, which is often the difference between a founder stuck in operations and a founder back on growth work.

How Columbus's Time Zone Works With India

Columbus runs on Eastern Time. India Standard Time is 9 hours 30 minutes ahead of ET. A Columbus 8am to 5pm ET workday maps to 5:30pm to 2:30am IST. Our staff work that shift as their full-time hours, giving your Columbus team live coverage during business hours.
