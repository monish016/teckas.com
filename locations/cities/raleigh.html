Why Raleigh Businesses Hire Remote

Raleigh-Durham is the original research-park city. Research Triangle Park, anchored between Raleigh, Durham, and Chapel Hill, hosts GlaxoSmithKline, Pfizer, IQVIA, Biogen, Fidelity, Cisco, IBM, Red Hat (now IBM), and Epic Games - roughly 50,000 jobs inside the park alone, plus the surrounding biotech and SaaS ecosystem. Duke, UNC, and NC State feed an unusually deep pool of PhDs, software engineers, and research scientists. The region's combined population grew roughly 20 percent between 2010 and 2020 per US Census.

The problem for seed-stage and bootstrapped Triangle startups is that this deep talent pool is also expensive. Per Built In RDU and Glassdoor Raleigh-Durham 2024-2025 data, mid-level software engineers now command $110,000-$150,000 base, and scientific ops roles at small biotechs push into the same range. Those are manageable rates for GSK and Pfizer. They are not manageable for a pre-seed therapeutics startup or a Series A SaaS operator.

Raleigh remote staffing through Teckas lets Triangle founders build out engineering pods, QA coverage, scientific back-office, and CX at one-fourth to one-fifth of loaded local cost - while keeping PhDs, senior engineers, and regulatory leads in Cary, Morrisville, or downtown Durham.

How Raleigh's Time Zone Works With India

Raleigh runs on Eastern Time. India Standard Time is 9 hours 30 minutes ahead of ET. A Raleigh 9am to 6pm ET workday maps to 6:30pm to 3:30am IST in India. Our Triangle-aligned staff work that window as their full-time shift, so your engineers and scientists get live coverage during RTP business hours.
