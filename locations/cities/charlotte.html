Why Charlotte Businesses Hire Remote

Charlotte is the second-largest banking center in the United States by total assets, after only New York. Bank of America is headquartered here. Truist was formed by the merger of BB&T and SunTrust and runs a massive Charlotte footprint. Wells Fargo operates its largest east-coast hub in the city. Honeywell moved its corporate headquarters to Uptown. Duke Energy and LendingTree anchor the non-bank financial base. A growing fintech ecosystem - payments, wealth tech, insurance tech - has developed around the big banks.

For Charlotte SMBs, RIAs, fintech startups, and professional services firms, that concentration has a cost. Operations, finance, and admin salaries in Charlotte have climbed roughly 20-25 percent since 2020 per Indeed and Glassdoor Charlotte MSA data, as mid-market employers compete with the big banks for the same pool of ops and analyst talent. An RIA running a 10-person team in SouthPark cannot afford to bid against Bank of America for a client-service admin.

Charlotte remote staffing through Teckas gives finance-adjacent operators trained, full-time team members in Eastern Time at one-third of loaded local cost, while keeping relationship managers, advisors, and bankers in-market where they need to be.

How Charlotte's Time Zone Works With India

Charlotte runs on Eastern Time. India Standard Time is 9 hours 30 minutes ahead of ET. A Charlotte 8am to 5pm ET workday maps to 5:30pm to 2:30am IST in India. Our staff work that shift as their full-time hours.
