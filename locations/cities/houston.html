Why Houston Businesses Hire Remote

Houston is a city built on three massive and very different economies: energy, medicine, and logistics. ExxonMobil, Chevron, Shell USA, and hundreds of oilfield services firms run headquarters and back offices out of Houston. The Texas Medical Center is the largest medical complex in the world, employing over 120,000 people across 60+ institutions. The Port of Houston moves more foreign tonnage than any other US port. Stacked on top of that, Houston added roughly 200,000 residents between 2020 and 2024, driving a home-services boom across HVAC, plumbing, and roofing that rivals any metro in the country.

That mix creates a specific staffing problem. Energy bust cycles make it painful to carry full-time admin and support headcount when oil is at $60 a barrel, and equally painful to hire fast enough when it is at $90. Home-service operators in Katy, Sugar Land, The Woodlands, and Pearland are running on 30-40 trucks and cannot find dispatchers and CSRs fast enough. Medical practices are burning hours on billing and prior-auth that does not need to sit inside the clinic. Houston remote staffing through Teckas solves that by giving you a trained, full-time team member who works your Central Time hours without the volatility of a W-2 hire.

Houston also has no state income tax, but loaded local salaries for operations roles have climbed 18-22 percent since 2019 per Indeed and BLS Houston metro data. Payroll pressure is real even without Austin-style tech inflation.

How Houston's Time Zone Works With India

Houston runs on Central Time year-round, with standard DST observance. India Standard Time is 10 hours 30 minutes ahead of CT during standard time and 10 hours 30 minutes ahead during daylight saving. A Houston 8am to 5pm CT shift maps to roughly 6:30pm to 3:30am IST in India, and our staff are hired specifically for that night shift as their primary work window. They are not moonlighting across time zones. When your Houston service manager calls at 2pm, your dispatcher is online and answering in real time.
