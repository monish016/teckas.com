Why New York Businesses Are Going Remote

New York has the highest cost-of-living of any US state, driven almost entirely by New York City. BLS OES shows NYC metro wages running 25 to 50 percent above the national median across most white-collar occupations, and that is before rent-loaded hybrid commute subsidies, New York State and New York City payroll taxes, and mandatory paid family and sick leave. A headcount decision in Manhattan is fundamentally different from one in Atlanta or Dallas: every role carries real estate overhead per square foot, and turnover is expensive because talent gets poached by finance firms willing to pay more.

Since 2021, most NYC employers have settled into hybrid or fully-remote models, which has made offshore augmentation a natural extension, not a culture shift. Finance ops teams, ecommerce and DTC brands, professional services firms, and agencies in Manhattan and Brooklyn use Teckas to run the non-senior layer of their team from India on Eastern Time. The result is lower cost per role and the ability to staff overflow and coverage shifts without signing another office lease.

How Working in New York Eastern Time Works

New York runs on Eastern Time. India is 9.5 to 10.5 hours ahead. A 9am to 6pm ET NYC shift becomes a 6:30pm to 3:30am IST shift in India, and we hire dedicated teammates specifically for that schedule. Your Teckas staffer is online for your morning stand-up, your mid-day fire drills, and your end-of-day hand-off, not just a couple of overlap hours. For finance ops clients handling US market data, we can also run an earlier shift to cover pre-market and market-open windows.

Compliance and Employment Notes

New York State has aggressive worker-protection laws (paid family leave, paid sick leave, WARN Act, pay transparency) and some of the highest payroll tax exposure in the country. None of that applies to Teckas staff because they are employees of Teckas Technologies Pvt. Ltd. in India, not New York-based employees or contractors. You pay a monthly service fee, we handle Indian payroll, statutory contributions, HR, and equipment. No W-2, no 1099, no NYS-45 filings. For regulated finance clients we can sign NDAs, SCCs, and DPAs on request.
