Why NC Banking Firms Hire Remote Admins

Charlotte is the second-largest banking center in the United States by assets under management, behind only New York. Bank of America is headquartered in Charlotte, Truist (formed from BB&T and SunTrust) is headquartered there, and Wells Fargo has a major East Coast footprint in the city. Add the dense fintech and servicing cluster, plus the community bank and credit union base across Raleigh, Greensboro, and Winston-Salem, and North Carolina concentrates a disproportionate share of US financial services ops. Those operations all run loan ops, KYC/BSA, deposit services, and servicing queues that need reliable admin capacity.

Charlotte banking admin pay per Glassdoor runs $52,000 to $68,000, or $4,500 to $5,800 loaded. Loan processor and BSA analyst pay is higher. A Teckas banking admin works Eastern Time inside your core system and document management platform, under strict confidentiality controls.

BLS May 2023 national median for loan interviewers and clerks is $47,650.
