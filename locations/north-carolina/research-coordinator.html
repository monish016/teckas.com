Why RTP Biotech Companies Hire Remote Research Coordinators

Research Triangle Park is home to IQVIA, Parexel, PPD (now Thermo Fisher), Syneos Health, and Labcorp Drug Development (now Fortrea), among the world's largest CRO operators. Sponsors like Biogen, Eli Lilly, Merck, and hundreds of emerging biotechs run clinical programs through RTP teams. Trial operations require enormous amounts of documentation management, query resolution, source data review, eTMF upkeep, and site communication. Much of that work does not require patient contact and happens entirely inside CTMS and EDC systems.

RTP clinical research coordinator pay per Glassdoor runs $58,000 to $78,000 per year, or $5,500 to $7,500 loaded. Experienced CDAs and CRAs cost substantially more. A Teckas research coordinator works your Eastern Time hours inside Medidata Rave, Veeva CTMS, Florence, or Oracle Clinical One, handling the documentation-heavy side of study ops.

All our coordinators complete GCP training before placement.
