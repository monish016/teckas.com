Why Ontario SaaS and Tech Hire Remote Developers

Ontario is the largest tech province in Canada. Toronto-Waterloo is one of the top five tech corridors in North America per the CBRE Scoring Tech Talent report, with Shopify (Ottawa HQ, significant Toronto footprint), OpenText, Constellation Software, Wealthsimple, Clio Canada, Ada, and hundreds of venture-backed SaaS companies. Waterloo's University of Waterloo and Laurier pipeline plus the RIM/BlackBerry legacy creates a dense mid-level engineer pool, but post-2021 the wages climbed materially. Levels.fyi data puts Toronto and Waterloo mid-level engineers at CAD $110,000 to $150,000 base, with senior engineers at Shopify and Wealthsimple clearing CAD $180,000. Loaded with CPP, EI, EHT, statutory vacation, and extended health, a single seat is CAD $13,000 to $17,000 per month.

For a Series A or B Ontario SaaS extending runway, a Teckas remote developer in India working Eastern Time overlap is a material cost saver. The engagement is straightforward: invoicing happens in USD, no Canadian payroll or CRA filings, no Ontario ESA compliance risk.

BLS-equivalent Canadian source data (Statistics Canada NOC 21232) tracks software engineer earnings near CAD $110,000+ median.
