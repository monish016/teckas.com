Why Ontario Businesses Hire Remote CS Agents

Ontario hosts over 40 percent of Canada's economy, anchored by Toronto's financial and tech cluster and Ottawa's public sector and software companies. The Ontario SMB and scaling startup base is enormous per Innovation, Science and Economic Development Canada data, and those operators all face the same support cost problem. Toronto CSR pay per Glassdoor runs CAD $48,000 to $62,000 per year, or CAD $5,500 to $7,200 loaded once you include CPP, EI, EHT, and extended health. Adding Ontario ESA statutory holidays and vacation on top makes a 40-hour support seat pricier than most growing brands expect.

A Teckas remote CS agent is a full-time Teckas India employee working Eastern Time, inside your Zendesk, Gorgias, or Intercom. Your engagement with Teckas is a vendor relationship invoiced in USD. You do not run Ontario payroll, file T4s, or track Ontario ESA compliance on them.

Statistics Canada NOC 64409 (customer service representatives) tracks near CAD $20 per hour median.
