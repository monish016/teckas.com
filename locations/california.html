Why California Businesses Are Going Remote

California has the highest fully loaded cost of labor of any US state. The Bureau of Labor Statistics ranks San Francisco, San Jose, and Los Angeles among the top metros nationally for mean wage. Even non-technical roles (customer support, bookkeeping, coordinators) routinely clear $75,000 to $95,000 loaded in the Bay Area, and entry-level SaaS engineers are quoted between $140K and $180K per Levels.fyi and Glassdoor data. Layer on California employer taxes, paid leave mandates, and commercial rent, and the true cost of a local hire typically lands 25 to 35 percent above the headline salary.

That math is why California is Teckas's largest US market. Series A and B SaaS startups in SF and LA use us to extend runway by 6 to 9 months. Ecommerce and DTC brands in LA use remote CX, ops, and creative production staff. Real estate and property management groups across San Diego and Sacramento run leasing, transaction coordination, and back-office through Teckas. The pattern is consistent: California teams keep the senior strategic hires local and push repeatable operational roles to trained remote staff.

How Working in California Pacific Time Works

California runs on Pacific Time year-round (PT). India is 12.5 to 13.5 hours ahead. A 9am to 6pm PT workday is a 9:30pm to 6:30am IST shift. That sounds rough, but it is our largest staffing shift, and we hire specifically for it. Our California-shift staff live on PT rhythm: they are online during California stand-ups, on Slack and Zoom the same hours as your team, and they sleep during California evenings. You are not getting a contractor doing a few hours of overlap - you are getting a full-time teammate on your calendar.

Compliance and Employment Notes

California is the most regulated labor state in the country (AB5, PAGA, meal-break rules, CCPA, CPRA). None of those apply to Teckas staff because they are not California employees or California contractors. They are full-time employees of Teckas Technologies Pvt. Ltd. under Indian labor law, and you engage Teckas via a monthly service agreement. That structure avoids AB5 classification, removes California payroll-tax exposure, and puts HR, benefits, and termination handling on us. For compliance-sensitive California clients, we can sign BAAs and standard DPAs (GDPR and CCPA aligned) on request.
