Why North Carolina Businesses Are Going Remote

North Carolina has two of the hottest job markets in the Southeast. Charlotte is the second-largest banking center in the US after New York, with Bank of America, Truist, and Wells Fargo all operating major regional HQs there. The Research Triangle (Raleigh, Durham, Chapel Hill) has the country's highest density of PhDs relative to population, and the surrounding biotech and SaaS ecosystem (Red Hat, Fidelity's Raleigh campus, SAS, Pendo, and a large biotech cluster anchored by Duke, UNC, and NC State) is bidding up tech compensation toward Austin and Atlanta levels per Built In RDU and Levels.fyi data.

At the same time, NC's working-age labor force has tightened as in-migration pushed housing costs up and priced out support-role hiring in the Triangle and Charlotte corridors. Teckas supports NC operators by running the non-strategic portion of their team on Eastern Time from India. Charlotte finance clients use us for ops analyst and reconciliation support. RTP SaaS and biotech operators use us for developers, QA, and clinical admin support.

How Working in North Carolina Eastern Time Works

NC runs on Eastern Time. India is 9.5 to 10.5 hours ahead. A 9am to 6pm ET shift is 6:30pm to 3:30am IST. We hire dedicated teammates for that shift.

Compliance and Employment Notes

North Carolina is an employer-friendly state with no state-specific contractor reclassification laws. Teckas staff are employees of Teckas Technologies Pvt. Ltd. in India. For banking and biotech clients we sign NDAs, DPAs, BAAs (for HIPAA), and standard SCCs on request.
