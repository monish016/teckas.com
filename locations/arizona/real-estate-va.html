Why Arizona Agents Hire Remote Real Estate VAs

Phoenix has been one of the fastest-growing real estate markets in the US for most of the past decade. Maricopa County has added more residents than almost any other county in the country per Census data, and ARMLS consistently processes some of the highest listing and transaction volumes of any MLS in the US. Scottsdale luxury, Phoenix metro suburbs, and Tucson retirement and second-home buyers all create a steady pipeline. For growing teams doing 50 to 300 transactions per year, the listing-input, CMA, lead-follow-up, and social content work piles up fast.

Local Phoenix real estate assistant pay runs $42,000 to $55,000 per year per Indeed, and loaded costs $3,600 to $4,600 per month. Most team owners would rather pay their agents to sell and pay a remote VA to handle admin. Arizona also has specific contract paperwork under the Arizona Association of Realtors (AAR) contracts, which requires focused TC training rather than generic admin skill.

A Teckas real estate VA works your Mountain Time shift inside ARMLS, Dotloop, and Follow Up Boss. BLS May 2023 median for secretaries and admins (except legal/medical/executive) is $44,080.
