# Link-Building Outreach Templates

Use these to pitch the 2026 Salary Report and other assets to HR, staffing, and business publications for backlinks. Customize bracketed fields.

**Pro tip:** Personalize the first line of every outreach email with something specific about the recipient's recent post/article/publication. Generic pitches have <2% response rate. Personalized pitches hit 8-15%.

## Outreach Cadence

Best practice for cold email outreach:

- **Email 1 (Day 1):** The pitch (templates above)
- **Email 2 (Day 5):** Short bump - "Did my email get lost? Following up on X."
- **Email 3 (Day 12):** Different angle - pitch a different topic or resource
- **Stop after 3.** Further follow-up hurts deliverability.

## Tracking

Create a simple spreadsheet:
| Target | Contact | Date Sent | Reply | Published | Backlink URL |

Goal: 10-20 outreach emails per week. Expect 5-15% response rate. Realistic to land 2-5 backlinks per month in the first 6 months.

## Paid Alternatives (if outreach is too slow)

- **HARO / Qwoted / SourceBottle** - free, journalists post requests, you respond. Highest ROI for time.
- **Featured.com** - paid ($99/mo), similar to HARO
- **Terkel.io** - paid, expert round-ups
- **PRnewswire / Businesswire** - paid press release distribution ($300-800) for the Salary Report launch

A single $500 press release can earn 20-50 syndicated backlinks from low-mid authority sites. Won't move the needle on its own but provides a quick credibility floor.
