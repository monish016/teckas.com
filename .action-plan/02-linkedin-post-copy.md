## Posting Tips

1. **Timing**: Post Tuesday-Thursday between 9-11am ET for best reach
2. **First comment trick**: LinkedIn de-prioritizes posts with external links in the body. Putting the URL in the first comment typically gets 2-3x more reach.
3. **Engage for the first hour**: Reply to every comment in the first 60 minutes. LinkedIn's algorithm rewards early engagement.
4. **Tag 2-3 people** who might find it useful in your first comment (not the post - looks spammy in the post)
5. **Image**: Attach a screenshot of one of the salary comparison tables. Visual posts get 2x engagement.
