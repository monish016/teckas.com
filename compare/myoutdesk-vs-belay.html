MyOutDesk vs BELAY: Full Comparison

A neutral 2026 comparison of two US-based remote staffing brands with very different positioning: Philippines-based real estate specialization vs US-based premium onshore VAs.

TL;DR Summary

MyOutDesk and BELAY are both well-established US-based remote staffing companies but they sit at opposite ends of the market on talent region and price. MyOutDesk places Philippines-based dedicated professionals with deep real estate specialization and broader industry coverage. BELAY places US-based contractors as premium onshore virtual assistants, bookkeepers, and specialists. Buyers usually choose MyOutDesk for cost-effective specialized support, and BELAY for onshore cultural alignment at a premium.

MyOutDesk Overview

MyOutDesk is a long-running US-based provider of Philippines-based virtual professionals. It is widely recognized for real estate specialization, with deep experience placing Inside Sales Agents, transaction coordinators, marketing assistants, and admin support for brokerages, teams, and top-producing agents. The company also serves clients outside real estate across general admin, customer support, and operations.

The company's pitch centers on vertical depth for real estate and cost-effective managed staffing across other industries. Pricing is subscription-based, with role and seniority tiers. Clients get a dedicated full-time professional, onboarding support, and ongoing account management. The model fits companies that want to add full-time capacity without onshore cost.

Strengths:

  • Deep real estate specialization; strong ISA and TC playbooks
  • Long track record serving US brokerages and teams
  • Philippines-based talent at meaningfully lower cost than onshore alternatives
  • Dedicated full-time placements with account management

Weaknesses:

  • Philippines-only talent region
  • Vertical specialization strongest in real estate; less deep in other niches
  • Time zone alignment depends on shift planning; client needs to be intentional about coverage windows

BELAY Overview

BELAY is a US-based remote staffing company known for placing US-based contractors as executive assistants, bookkeepers, social media managers, and website specialists. It targets founders, executives, ministries, nonprofits, and growing businesses that prioritize onshore cultural alignment and native English. BELAY handles matching, onboarding, and ongoing success, and has built a strong brand among US executives looking for a carefully matched assistant.

Because talent is US-based, BELAY sits at the premium end of the VA market. Clients are not buying savings; they are buying onshore proximity, native English, full US time-zone coverage, and a highly curated match process. That combination works especially well for executives who need a long-term, consistent support relationship.

Strengths:

  • US-based contractors; full time-zone and cultural alignment
  • Careful matching process across specialized roles
  • Strong brand recognition among US executives and nonprofits
  • Managed account support and ongoing relationship management

Weaknesses:

  • Premium pricing; not a cost-savings play
  • Plan-hour caps may not fit heavy, variable workloads
  • Narrower role catalog than multi-region staffing firms

Detailed Comparison by Category

Pricing Model

MyOutDesk is subscription priced with tiers based on role and seniority, and is generally well below US-onshore pricing because the talent is Philippines-based. BELAY is also subscription priced but at a premium level, reflecting US contractor rates. On a dollars-per-equivalent-hour basis, MyOutDesk is typically a material savings over BELAY for similar role scopes.

Talent Quality and Region

MyOutDesk's talent pool is Philippines-based and well suited to English-heavy real estate and general admin roles. BELAY's talent pool is US-based and well suited to executive work where cultural proximity and same-timezone availability matter most. Both are strong in their respective regions; the comparison is less about "quality" and more about which region fits the role.

Role Coverage

MyOutDesk's role catalog is anchored in real estate (ISA, TC, marketing VA, admin) and extends into general admin, customer support, and marketing operations across other industries. BELAY's catalog is narrower and more premium, centered on executive assistants, bookkeepers, social media managers, and web specialists. For a real estate team, MyOutDesk's catalog is harder to beat; for a premium onshore EA or bookkeeper, BELAY is a more natural fit.

Management Approach

Both companies invest in account management and matching. MyOutDesk layers in vertical training, particularly for real estate workflows. BELAY layers in careful matching and ongoing relationship support aimed at long-term EA placements. Both offer replacement if a placement does not work, with both reputations being solid on that dimension.

Commitment and Flexibility

Both operate on monthly subscriptions. MyOutDesk engagements tend to be long-running because of the depth of integration with brokerage and team systems. BELAY engagements also tend to be long-running because executives rarely want to re-match their assistant frequently. Neither is designed for one-off or highly variable work.

Who MyOutDesk Is Best For

MyOutDesk is a strong fit for:

  • Real estate brokerages, teams, and top-producing agents
  • Companies comfortable with Philippines-based full-time support
  • Buyers who need a trained ISA or transaction coordinator at scale
  • Businesses wanting material cost savings over onshore providers
  • Teams that can staff coverage windows around Philippines time zones

Who BELAY Is Best For

BELAY is a strong fit for:

  • US executives and founders wanting onshore executive assistance
  • Nonprofits and ministries prioritizing cultural alignment
  • Buyers needing specialized US-based bookkeeping or social media help
  • Teams willing to pay premium onshore rates for consistency
  • Leaders who want a carefully matched long-term assistant

Frequently Asked Questions

Is MyOutDesk only for real estate?
No. MyOutDesk serves a broader set of industries, but it is most widely known for real estate specialization and has a long history with brokerages, agents, and teams. Its non-real-estate practice includes admin, customer support, marketing, and operations roles.
Is BELAY more expensive than MyOutDesk?
Generally yes. BELAY uses US-based contractors and sits in the premium onshore VA tier, which tends to price higher than Philippines-based placements. MyOutDesk places Philippines-based professionals at subscription rates that are lower than typical onshore VA pricing. Exact totals depend on role, seniority, and plan.
Which is better for an executive assistant role?
BELAY is strongly positioned for onshore executive assistant placements where US time-zone coverage and native English are priorities. MyOutDesk can also provide executive assistant support with Philippines talent at a lower price, which is a strong fit for buyers comfortable with offshore support. The right choice depends on cost sensitivity and preference for onshore vs offshore.
What if I want an India-based managed hire instead?
Neither MyOutDesk nor BELAY sources from India. Buyers who specifically want India-based professionals, especially for engineering, finance, analytics, or senior back-office roles, typically evaluate India-focused managed staffing providers such as Teckas. The model is managed and flat monthly, similar in spirit to MyOutDesk but priced and staffed differently.
