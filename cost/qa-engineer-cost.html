Direct Answer: What a QA Engineer Costs

A QA engineer typically costs $75,000 to $110,000 per year in the US per BLS and Glassdoor 2026 data, or $100,000 to $155,000 fully loaded with benefits and overhead. Senior SDETs (software development engineers in test) run $120,000 to $160,000 base. Through managed remote staffing from India, a dedicated QA engineer costs $2,000 to $2,800 per month all-in - $24,000 to $33,600 per year fully loaded.

QA engineer cost is one of the largest hidden line items in any product engineering budget because quality debt is invisible until it is expensive. The right question is not "what does a QA engineer cost" but "what does shipping without a QA engineer cost in rework, incidents, and lost user trust." This guide walks through QA tester salary ranges, outsource QA testing cost options, and the real TCO of each model, using BLS, Glassdoor, Indeed, Stack Overflow Developer Survey, and Naukri data.

Hourly vs Full-Time QA Pricing

Hourly QA contractors. US manual QA: $40 to $65 per hour. US automation QA: $75 to $150 per hour. Offshore: $15 to $50 per hour. Good for release-gate testing bursts, regression test suite development, or one-off performance testing projects.

Crowd-testing platforms (uTest, Applause, Testbirds). $5 to $25 per tester-hour plus platform fees. Best for compatibility testing across many device/OS permutations. Weak for deep functional coverage.

Dedicated full-time QA engineer. $2,000 to $2,800 per month from India, $8,000 to $13,000 per month fully loaded in the US. The right choice for any team shipping weekly or faster. Compounds value because test assets (suites, fixtures, CI gating) appreciate over time.

What Drives QA Engineer Cost

Automation ratio. Pure manual QA is cheapest. Mixed manual+automation (writes Cypress or Playwright suites) is the sweet spot for most product teams. Full SDET (builds frameworks, owns CI test infrastructure, shift-left) commands the highest rates.

Domain. Web app QA is baseline. Mobile, security, and performance QA price 20 to 40 percent above baseline. Embedded, IoT, and medical device QA command the biggest premium due to regulatory requirements (FDA, IEC 62304).

Tooling. Selenium, Cypress, Playwright, Appium, Postman - all baseline. Add LoadRunner, k6, Burp Suite, or custom framework development and rates rise 25 to 40 percent.

Shift coverage. 24/5 or 24/7 follow-the-sun QA adds 30 to 60 percent per seat in every region.

Hidden Costs in Local Hiring

  • Benefits and payroll overhead (30-40%). Standard in the US.
  • Recruitment. QA recruiter fees 18 to 22 percent of first-year salary for automation roles. Time-to-fill averages 38 to 55 days.
  • Turnover. QA engineer tenure averages 2 to 3 years in the US. Replacement cost runs 50 to 100 percent of salary, with outsized productivity hit because test assets are under-documented.
  • Tooling. BrowserStack/Sauce Labs ($150 to $500 per seat per month), TestRail ($40 to $80 per seat per month), LoadRunner/k6 Cloud subscriptions, mobile device farms.
  • Opportunity cost of delay. Lost velocity while positions sit open, compounded by bug debt accumulating during hiring windows.

Common Questions

How much does a QA engineer cost per year?
US QA engineers earn $75,000 to $110,000 per year per BLS and Glassdoor 2026 data, with senior SDETs at $120,000 to $160,000. Fully loaded with benefits and overhead, US QA TCO runs $100,000 to $155,000. A dedicated QA engineer from India through Teckas costs $2,000 to $2,800 per month all-in, or $24,000 to $33,600 per year fully loaded.
Manual QA vs automation QA: which costs more?
Automation QA engineers (SDETs) cost 40 to 70 percent more than manual QA testers in every region. US manual QA: $55,000 to $75,000 base. US automation QA/SDET: $95,000 to $140,000 base. India manual QA via Teckas: $1,400 to $1,800 per month. India automation QA via Teckas: $2,000 to $2,800 per month.
What does outsourcing QA testing cost?
Per-hour QA outsourcing runs $15 to $50 per hour in offshore locations and $40 to $120 per hour in the US. Dedicated offshore QA engineers run $1,400 to $2,800 per month from India. For sustained testing needs, dedicated is 40 to 60 percent cheaper per hour than project-based hourly.
What tools should a QA engineer know?
Manual QA: Jira, TestRail, Zephyr, Postman, Chrome DevTools, BrowserStack. Automation QA: Selenium, Cypress, Playwright, Appium, Jest, TestNG, Rest Assured, plus CI tools (GitHub Actions, Jenkins, CircleCI). Performance and security specialists add JMeter, k6, OWASP ZAP, Burp Suite.
Is it worth hiring a full-time QA engineer for a small team?
For teams shipping weekly or faster with more than 3 developers, yes. A dedicated QA engineer typically catches 40 to 60 percent of regressions before release and removes 3 to 6 hours per week of testing time from each developer. At offshore rates ($2,000 to $2,800 per month), payback is under 60 days for most teams.
