FAQ

Common Questions from Real Estate Teams

Can a remote VA handle lead follow-up calls and appointment setting?
Yes. Our VAs are trained on real estate lead scripts and can handle inbound and outbound calls, qualify leads, and book appointments directly into your calendar. They work within your CRM and follow your team's specific qualification criteria.
Do your transaction coordinators understand US real estate contracts?
Absolutely. Our TCs are trained on US residential real estate transaction workflows, common contract forms, and state-specific requirements. They manage deadlines, coordinate with title companies and lenders, and ensure nothing falls through the cracks.
Will they work with my existing CRM like Follow Up Boss or KVCore?
Yes. Our professionals are experienced with all major real estate CRMs including Follow Up Boss, KVCore, BoomTown, Chime, and LionDesk. We match you with candidates who already know your specific platform.
How does time zone difference work for real estate?
We schedule your remote team to overlap with your core business hours. For lead follow-up, the time zone advantage actually helps because your VA can respond to morning web leads before your local agents even start their day.
