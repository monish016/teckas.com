FAQ

Common Questions from Manufacturers

Can remote staff work with our ERP system?
Yes. Our professionals are experienced with major ERP platforms including SAP, Oracle, NetSuite, Epicor, and Microsoft Dynamics. We match candidates who already have experience with your specific system to minimize training time.
Do they understand manufacturing procurement processes?
Absolutely. Our procurement specialists understand manufacturing-specific workflows including RFQ processes, vendor qualification, lead time management, MOQ negotiations, and multi-tier supply chain coordination. They work within your procurement guidelines and approval hierarchies.
Can they help with ISO and quality compliance documentation?
Yes. Our quality documentation specialists are experienced with ISO 9001, ISO 14001, and other quality management standards. They maintain document control systems, prepare audit documentation, track corrective actions, and keep your QMS records current and audit-ready.
How do they coordinate with our plant floor team?
Our remote professionals communicate with your on-site team through your existing communication tools such as email, Teams, Slack, and phone. They handle the administrative and documentation side while your plant floor team focuses on production. Most manufacturers find this division of labor immediately improves both productivity and accuracy.
What about proprietary manufacturing data security?
We take data security seriously. All professionals sign comprehensive NDAs, work on secured connections, and follow your company's data access policies. We can accommodate additional requirements like background checks, restricted system access, and compliance with your specific security protocols.
