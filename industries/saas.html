FAQ

Common Questions from SaaS Companies

What tech stacks do your developers work with?
Our developer pool covers all major stacks including React, Angular, Vue.js, Node.js, Python/Django, Ruby on Rails, PHP/Laravel, Java/Spring, and more. We also have specialists in mobile development (React Native, Flutter) and cloud infrastructure (AWS, GCP, Azure).
How do remote developers integrate with our agile workflow?
Our developers are experienced with agile and scrum methodologies. They attend standups, participate in sprint planning and retrospectives, and use tools like Jira, Linear, and GitHub. Most clients find remote developers integrate smoothly within the first sprint.
Can we do technical interviews before hiring?
Absolutely. After our initial vetting (which includes technical assessments and coding challenges), we present shortlisted candidates for your own technical interviews. You have full control over the final hiring decision. We want you to be 100% confident in every hire.
How do you handle IP protection and code security?
All our professionals sign comprehensive NDAs and IP assignment agreements. Developers work on secured environments, follow your security protocols, and use your version control systems. We can accommodate additional security requirements like background checks and device management policies.
What about time zone overlap for standups and collaboration?
India Standard Time provides a natural 4 to 5 hour overlap with US business hours (morning IST = evening EST). Many SaaS teams find this works well for async workflows with daily standups during the overlap window. We can also arrange shifted schedules for maximum overlap.
