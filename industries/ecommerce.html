FAQ

Common Questions from Ecommerce Brands

Can your agents handle Shopify, Amazon, and other marketplace platforms?
Absolutely. Our agents are experienced with Shopify, Amazon Seller Central, WooCommerce, BigCommerce, and other major platforms. We match you with candidates who already have hands-on experience with your specific tech stack.
How do you handle peak season staffing like Black Friday?
We can ramp up additional support agents ahead of peak seasons with as little as two weeks' notice. Many of our ecommerce clients scale from 2 to 5 or more agents during Q4, then scale back down. Our month-to-month model makes this flexible and cost-effective.
Will your team integrate with our helpdesk tools like Gorgias or Zendesk?
Yes. Our agents are trained on all major helpdesk and CRM platforms including Gorgias, Zendesk, Freshdesk, Intercom, and HubSpot. They will work within your existing workflows and follow your established SOPs and macros.
Can they handle returns and refund processing?
Yes. Our customer support agents are trained in end-to-end order management including returns processing, refund approvals within your guidelines, exchanges, and warranty claims. You set the policies, and they execute them consistently.
Do you provide 24/7 coverage?
We can arrange shift coverage that spans your busiest hours or provides full 24/7 support. The India time zone naturally provides coverage during US evenings and early mornings, and we can set up overlapping shifts to cover all hours.
