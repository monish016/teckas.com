FAQ

Common Questions from Law Firms

How do you ensure confidentiality and data security for legal matters?
All our legal professionals sign comprehensive NDAs and confidentiality agreements. They work on secured systems with VPN access and follow strict data handling protocols. We can comply with your firm's specific security requirements and ethical obligations.
Can remote paralegals do legal research on US law?
Yes. Our paralegal assistants are experienced with US legal research using Westlaw, LexisNexis, and other legal databases. They can perform case law research, statutory analysis, and prepare research memos under attorney supervision.
Will they integrate with our practice management software?
Absolutely. Our professionals are experienced with Clio, MyCase, PracticePanther, Filevine, and other major legal practice management platforms. We match candidates based on your specific technology stack.
Can they handle court filing and deadline management?
Our legal secretaries and paralegal assistants are trained in deadline tracking and can manage court calendars, filing deadlines, and statute of limitations tracking. They work within your existing docketing systems to ensure nothing is missed.
