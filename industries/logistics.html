FAQ

Common Questions from Logistics Companies

Can remote staff handle real-time freight dispatching?
Yes. Our freight dispatchers work within your TMS and load board platforms in real-time. They track shipments, coordinate with carriers, and handle exceptions just like an on-site dispatcher would. The key difference is cost, not capability.
Do they have experience with our TMS platform?
Our professionals have experience with major TMS platforms including McLeod, TMW, MercuryGate, Descartes, and others. We match candidates based on your specific technology stack to minimize onboarding time.
How does the time zone work for logistics operations?
Logistics never sleeps, and neither does our talent. The India time zone provides natural coverage during US evening and overnight hours. For daytime operations, we schedule staff to overlap with your core business hours. Many 3PLs use this to extend their operating hours without overtime costs.
Can they communicate directly with our carriers and customers?
Absolutely. Our operations coordinators and dispatchers are fluent English communicators who handle carrier and customer calls professionally. They use your company phone systems and represent your brand seamlessly.
