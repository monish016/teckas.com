FAQ

Common Questions from Home Services Companies

Can a remote dispatcher handle real-time scheduling for field technicians?
Yes. Our dispatchers are trained on leading field service platforms like ServiceTitan, Housecall Pro, and Jobber. They manage real-time job assignments, route optimization, and technician communication using the same tools your in-house team would use. Many of our clients find remote dispatchers are actually more focused and efficient because they have fewer in-office distractions.
Will customers know they're speaking to someone overseas?
Our CSRs are fluent in English with neutral accents and are trained on home services phone scripts specific to your company. We conduct accent and communication assessments during our vetting process. Most customers will not notice any difference from a local representative.
How do your staff handle after-hours emergency calls?
Because our professionals are based in India, the time zone difference actually works in your favor. Your remote staff can cover evening and early-morning calls during their regular business hours, giving you extended coverage without paying overtime. We can set up shifts that align with your busiest call windows.
What if I already use ServiceTitan or Housecall Pro?
That is ideal. Our dispatchers and CSRs are experienced with all major field service management platforms. We will match you with professionals who already have hands-on experience with your specific software, so onboarding is faster and smoother.
How quickly can I have someone answering phones?
Most home services clients have their first hire onboarded and taking calls within 7 to 10 business days. We handle recruitment, vetting, and initial training. You provide your scripts, software access, and any company-specific procedures.
