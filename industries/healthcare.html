FAQ

Common Questions from Healthcare Practices

How do you handle HIPAA compliance with remote staff?
Our professionals are trained on HIPAA regulations and sign Business Associate Agreements. They work on secured devices with VPN connections and follow strict data handling protocols. We can work with your compliance team to meet your specific security requirements.
Are your billing specialists certified in medical coding?
Many of our billing specialists hold certifications in medical coding (CPC, CCS) or have equivalent experience. We match you with candidates who have specific experience in your specialty, whether it is primary care, orthopedics, dental, or any other field.
Can they work with our existing EHR system?
Yes. Our professionals are experienced with major EHR and practice management systems including Epic, athenahealth, AdvancedMD, Kareo, DrChrono, and Practice Fusion. We will match you with candidates who already know your specific platform.
Will patients interact directly with remote staff?
That depends on your preference. Many practices use remote staff for patient-facing tasks like scheduling calls and appointment reminders. Our coordinators are professional, empathetic, and trained in patient communication. You can also limit them to back-office tasks only.
