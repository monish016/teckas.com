FAQ

Common Questions from Agencies

Will the remote team member feel like part of our agency?
Yes. Our professionals work full-time and exclusively for your agency. They attend your standups, use your project management tools, and become embedded in your team culture. Most clients say their Teckas hires feel no different from in-house staff.
Can designers work within our existing brand systems and style guides?
Absolutely. Our designers are experienced in working within established design systems, brand guidelines, and component libraries. They are proficient in Figma, Adobe Creative Suite, and collaborative design workflows that agencies use daily.
How do you ensure code quality from remote developers?
Our developers are vetted through technical assessments, portfolio reviews, and live coding exercises. They follow your code review processes and Git workflows. You maintain full control over quality standards, and our developers are experienced with PR-based development processes.
Can we white-label these team members to our clients?
Yes. Your remote team members work under your agency brand. They can attend client meetings, use your agency email addresses, and represent your company. How you position your team to clients is entirely up to you.
