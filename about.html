Our Story

How Teckas Got Started

Teckas is a service brand of Teckas Technologies Pvt. Ltd.

Our founder spent years in digital marketing, building and scaling remote teams for agencies and startups across the US. During that time, one thing became painfully clear: small and mid-sized businesses were being priced out of great talent. The best people cost $70K, $80K, $90K a year - and that was before benefits, HR, and overhead.

Meanwhile, India had an enormous pool of highly skilled, English-fluent professionals who were eager to work with US companies but had no reliable way to connect. The talent gap wasn't about ability - it was about access. Freelance platforms were unreliable. Offshore agencies delivered inconsistent quality. And managing international hires on your own was a compliance nightmare.

That's why Teckas Technologies was built - to bridge this gap with a managed, turnkey solution. We don't just find you a hire and disappear. We recruit, vet, employ, equip, and manage your remote team members end-to-end. You get the talent. We handle everything else.

We built Teckas Technologies to connect businesses with exceptional remote talent from India. From one-person startups to established firms with growing remote teams, we help businesses of every size build reliable, high-performing remote teams - at a fraction of the local cost.
